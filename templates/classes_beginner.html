Beginner Classes

We recommend Beginner Yoga with Jane Johnson if this is your first time taking a Yoga class. You will the fundamentals of Yoga. Jane goes through the benefits of yoga as well as the history of Yoga. After the first class with Jane Johnson this class will be the weekly class for you! Here we will set up a plan on how to reach your goals! (You will also reach them in this class) Every Wednesday and Friday are beginner yoga for those who have had the first class with Jane Johnson and want to practice yoga more than once a week. This class will continue to help you reach your goal!
